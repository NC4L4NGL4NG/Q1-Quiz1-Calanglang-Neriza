Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen , is an anime that reveals sorcerer's with abilities to fight and have cursed energies. The main characters are Satoru Gojo, the strongest sorcerer but also a sensei for a young boy named Yuji Itadori. Something about Yuji was that he ate a finger of a character who was incarnated and whom know lives inside his body is known as Sukuna Ryomen.

Cast

  • "Satoru Gojo is one of the main protagonists of the Jujutsu Kaisen series. He is a special grade jujutsu sorcerer and widely recognized as the strongest in the world."

    Yuji Itadori is the main protagonist. He is the son of Jin Itadori and Kaori Itadori, and the grandson of Wasuke Itadori. Yuji was living a normal life in Sendai City until he encountered Megumi and ate one of Sukuna's fingers.

    Megumi Fushiguro is the deuteragonist. He is a grade 2 jujutsu sorcerer and first-year student at Tokyo Jujutsu High alongside Yuji Itadori and Nobara Kugisaki.

    Nobara Kugisaki is the tritagonist. She is a first-year student and grade 3 jujutsu sorcerer at Tokyo Jujutsu High studying under Satoru Gojo alongside Yuji and Megumi.

    Ryomen Sukuna, more often referred to as simply Sukuna is the strongest jujutsu sorcerer from over a thousand years ago. Regarded as the undisputed King of Curses. Sukuna is one of the primary antagonists of the Jujutsu Kaisen series.
